The Art of Sourdough

Our journey began with a simple sourdough starter and a passion for traditional bread-making techniques.

Twenty years ago, we cultivated our first sourdough starter from wild yeasts and organic flour. That same starter, lovingly maintained and fed daily, is the heart of every loaf we bake today.

We believe in the slow food movement - our breads ferment for 18-24 hours, developing complex flavors and natural digestibility that you simply can't rush. Each loaf is hand-shaped and baked in our stone hearth ovens.
